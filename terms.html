FAQs

What type of ingredients do you use in your gourmet pizzas?

We use ingredients such as San Marzano tomatoes, avocado oil dough, and GMO-free microgreens to create our chef-curated gourmet pizzas. We believe that great pizza starts with wholesome, real food. Our kitchen uses premium ingredients you can actually recognize and pronounce—like flour, sea salt, and heart-healthy avocado oil. We never use unnatural additives or hidden chemicals. Every ingredient is chosen because we value your health as much as we value great flavour.

Do you offer wine pairing suggestions with your pizzas?

Yes, we provide wine pairing suggestions to enhance your dining experience with our gourmet pizzas.

How can I place an order for delivery?

You can place an order for delivery through Uber Eats or DoorDash for our delicious gourmet pizzas.

Are your ingredients sustainable and fresh?

Yes, we focus on quality, sustainability, and freshness in selecting our ingredients to ensure the best pizza experience for our customers.

Do you offer customization options for the pizzas?

Currently, we do not offer customization options as our pizzas are chef-curated with premium ingredients and signature microgreen finishes.
