  • Privacy Policy

    • What Information Do We Get?

      When you visit our website, we don't ask for your name, address, or credit card. We just want to show you pictures of delicious pizza!

    • Cookies (Digital Crumbs)

      Our website uses small digital crumbs called "cookies" to help the site load faster and work correctly on your phone or computer.

    • Other Websites (Uber Eats & DoorDash)

      When you click a button to buy a pizza, you leave our website and go to Uber Eats or DoorDash. We do not see the information you give them (like your home address or bank card). They have their own rules, so make sure you check their privacy pages too!
